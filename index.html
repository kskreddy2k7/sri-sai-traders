About Sri Sai Traders

Sri Sai Traders has been serving the Srinagar Colony community in Kurnool for over 15 years. We are your one-stop shop for all disposable items, general store essentials, and fresh daily milk. From paper cups and carry bags to fresh milk delivered every morning — we have it all.

Run by K. Mohan Reddy, we pride ourselves on offering quality products at competitive wholesale and retail prices. Our customers trust us for our reliability, fair pricing, and the wide range of products we stock.
