About Sri Sai Traders

Sri Sai Traders is a trusted disposable items and general store located in Srinagar Colony B-Camp, Kurnool, Andhra Pradesh, India. With more than 10 years of experience, we proudly serve our customers with high-quality disposable products, general store items, and daily essentials.

We also provide fresh milk every day at our milk center. Milk is available morning 6:00 AM - 9:00 AM and evening 6:00 PM - 9:00 PM. Our shop is known for good quality products, fair prices, and reliable service. Both wholesale and retail purchases are available.
