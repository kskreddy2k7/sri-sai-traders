About Us

Serving Kurnool
With Pride

Sri Sai Traders is your trusted neighborhood store in Srinagar Colony B-Camp, Kurnool. We specialize in all kinds of disposable items — from paper cups and plates to cutlery and packaging — along with a full general store and a fresh milk center.

Under the ownership of K. Mohan Reddy, we are committed to providing high-quality products at fair prices, ensuring every customer leaves satisfied. Located opposite Shakuntala Kalaa Mandir Road, we are easily accessible to all residents and businesses in the area.
